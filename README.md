I created this project to play about with the trading212 API.

Key features:
- Breakdown of your portfolio & split between "personal" (anything NOT in pies) and "client" (anything in a pie)
- Visulisation of portfolio and % allocation of each asset
- Menu to play about with different allocation %'s and showing how much stock to buy/sell to hit targets
- Heatmap to visualise the sector concentration
- 3 Month price chart of owned stocks
--- PAGE 2 (DATA ANALYSIS) ---
- Request a CSV file of your investments over a 365 day period
- if you have requested a csv that you already have, we can find and access it rather than creating a new one
- Provide an editiable dataframe of your csv data
- Provide summaries of your csv data
